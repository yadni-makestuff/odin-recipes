Paneer Cheese Maggie

Description

Paneer cheese maggie is an indian noodle dish consisting of just 3 indgredients Maggie noodles,Panner or cottage cheese,and cheese it is a 5 minute recipe which results into a tasty comfort food on basically any occasion or season. This recipe is for one packet Maggie

Ingredients

  • Maggie noodels
  • Cottage cheese / paneer
  • Cheese cube

Steps

  1. Boil water: Take a pan or a small wok take two and half cups of water and boil it
  2. Add Spices: Add the spice packet in the maggie pocket
  3. Add Extra Spices: Add oil and salt and chilli powder turmuric
  4. Add Cheese: Add as much cheese as you'd like (there's never enough cheese)
  5. Add Paneer: Add the cottage cheese cube
  6. Serve: Garnish it with coriander and serve it
